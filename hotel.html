Hotel Info

The Hotel Carmel is the ~~official~~ wedding hotel. We're encouraging all out-of-town guests to stay there, because we think it will be fun to have everyone together in one place.

It's definitely not the most upscale hotel in Santa Monica, but it's one of the most well-located and affordable.

To book, go to hotelcarmel.com and use promo code RYZA23 to get the special block rate. If there are no rooms left of the kind you want, email or text one of us (skyewm@gmail.com or sandyryza@gmail.com) and we can expand the block.
