Transportation:

The closest airport is San Diego International Airport (SAN)

If you stay in Mission Beach or Pacific Beach, you may not need a car. The main events are within a 20-minute walking radius.

For Friday dinner, it is possible to take the 30 bus from Pacific Beach to UCSD and either walk or transfer to the 101 bus to the Gliderport -- but the easiest way is carpooling or taking a Lyft/Uber.

Stuff to do nearby:

  • Go to the beach
  • Go surfing at Tourmaline
  • Go for a bluff hike at Torrey Pines
  • Hang out at Belmont Park at the end of Mission Beach
  • Go to the museums or a performance in Balboa Park
  • Go to the San Diego Botanical Garden in Encinitas
  • See the seals and sea lions at La Jolla Cove
  • Snorkel with harmless leopard sharks at La Jolla Shores
  • See the outdoor sculpture collection at UCSD, and don't miss the brutalist classic Geisel (Dr. Seuss) Library
